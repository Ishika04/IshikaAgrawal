Hello!

My name is Ishika and I'm currently a senior at Cornell University studying Information Science with a concentration in Digital Culture & Production, along with a minor in business.

I'm a designer who codes. I'm a designer who codes. My experiences include being a UX Design Intern at Amazon where I worked on the FinTech team, co-founding a startup as a Product Design Intern at the Cornell Tech Policy Lab, and analyzing customer data as a Technical Advisor Intern at IBM.

I'm always excited for new opportunities to grow as a designer and collaborate with others to create beautiful digital experiences. Let's connect!
