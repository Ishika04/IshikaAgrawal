Hello!

My name is Ishika and I'm currently a junior at Cornell University studying Information Science with concentrations in UX Design and Data Science, along with a minor in business.

I'm a self-taught designer who codes. Currently, I'm a Technical Advisor Intern at IBM where I'm designing and building a data visualization dashboard, Product Design Intern at the Cornell Tech Policy Lab - where I co-founded a startup, and Product Research Lead at Hack4Impact where I focus on building tech solutions for non-profits.

I am passionate about listening to people's stories, solving problems, and creating beautiful designs that have an impact. I've been challenging myself to learn more and more about design by creating across platforms including the web, mobile devices, and virtual reality.

I'm always excited for new opportunities to grow as a designer and collaborate with others to create beautiful digital experiences. Let's connect!
